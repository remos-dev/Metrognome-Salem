Budget Discipline

The project completed within the original $815,000 construction budget envelope. While individual line items shifted significantly — driven by $33K in framing change orders, new scope for WiFi/fire alarm and acoustic insulation, and a tripling of the interest reserve — active reallocation from savings in architecture (-$27K), legal (-$14K), and eliminated pre-close items (-$59K) kept the total on target.

Contingency Management

Combined contingency of $134K was 92.9% consumed. Hard cost contingency absorbed $111K in change orders across 11 draws. While appropriately sized, the near-full utilization signals the project had minimal remaining buffer. Future projects of similar scope should consider a 25-30% HC contingency allocation for conversion work.

Interest & Reserve Exposure

The largest single variance was the interest reserve, which grew from $36K to $112K (+209%). This was funded by reallocating $59K from eliminated reimbursable expenses and savings across soft cost lines. The $36K remaining balance represents the only undisbursed amount in the project.

Investment Returns

At $1.74M total cost with $300K equity (17.2% LTC), the project underwrites to a 12.6% ROC, 24.1% stabilized cash-on-cash, and 65.5% equity IRR over a 10-year hold. Permanent refinance at a 6.03% rate with 1.5x DSCR generates ~$512K in equity cash-out, returning 171% of invested equity at refinance alone.
